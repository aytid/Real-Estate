About This Project

ICONIC'S RRR County is a prestigious 85-acre mega residential layout located at Choutuppal, right beside the proposed 300' Regional Ring Road. Developed by ICONIC INFRA GROUP, a name you can trust, this premium gated community offers open plots and villas with world-class amenities and excellent connectivity.

The project is strategically positioned to benefit from the rapid infrastructure development in the region, including the proposed Metro Rail, 8-Line Express Highway, and proximity to major pharmaceutical and industrial hubs.
